Red Queen Society | Apply

As a fellow of the Red Queen Society, we'll sponsor your reading habits. Any book or magazine you read will be paid for! No strings attached.

To apply, please email hello@redqueensociety.org with answers to the following questions. Feel free to get creative!

Don’t worry, these answers will be kept secret (read by our founders then permanently deleted) and you will not offend anyone, e.g. by listing “controversial” titles like Das Kapital or The Bell Curve.

  • Please list your interests and questions you often think about. Examples here and here.
  • What books do you want to buy?
  • What have you built?
  • If you could start a populist movement, what are some ideas you’d rally around?
  • Do you have a Straussian reading of any well-known literature?
  • What radical policy ideas are you interested in?
  • Who are the smartest people you personally know?

Thanks! We’ll review applications and get back to you.

You'll get 2 years as a part of the fellowship, though we’ll increase that to 3 years if you share this application on your main social media platform or send this link (redqueensociety.org/apply) to 5 or more friends within one week of applying. Who wouldn't love the chance for free books!
